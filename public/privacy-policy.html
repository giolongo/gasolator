Privacy Policy - Gasolator

Last Updated: 2026-06-16

1. Introduction

Gasolator ("we," "us," or "our") operates the Gasolator web application. This Privacy Policy explains our practices regarding the collection, use, and disclosure of information.

2. Information We Collect

We collect information you provide directly to us:

  • Route information (start location, destination, intermediate stops)
  • Vehicle consumption data
  • Fuel price information
  • Language preference

3. How We Use Information

We use the information you provide to:

  • Calculate your trip cost
  • Provide location and routing services
  • Display content in your preferred language
  • Improve our service

4. Google AdSense

We use Google AdSense to display advertisements. Google's use of advertising cookies enables it to serve ads based on your prior visits to our website and other sites. You may opt out of personalized advertising by visiting Google's Ads Settings.

5. Data Storage

Data entered into Gasolator is processed locally in your browser. We do not store your personal trip data on our servers without your explicit consent.

6. Third-Party Services

We use:

  • Nominatim for reverse geocoding
  • OSRM for route calculation
  • Google Maps API for mapping
  • Google AdSense for advertisements

7. Contact Us

For privacy-related questions, please contact us at: support@gasolator.com
